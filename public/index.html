About Ino'z

Ino'z is your go to restaurant for great priced all American food with a few twists!! We are open 7 days a week for lunch and dinner. We cater to everyone whether your looking for a relaxing outdoor environment on our patio, a view of Cypress Creek from our indoor sunroom, or more of a sports vibe in our main dining area with our 4 TVs and bar.

On our spacious lawn, take a relaxing stroll along the peaceful banks of Cypress Creek. We are a pet-friendly facility, allowing well-mannered, leashed fur babies on our outside deck and lawn areas.

Ino'z is more than just a great place to hang out, get consistently great food, and enjoy it in a great atmosphere. The staff at Ino'z is what sets us apart and makes us so special. Service is where we focus our priorities when it comes to caring for our customers. Courteous, prompt, efficient service done in a casual way is why Ino'z has thrived in Wimberley for over 16 years.

The staff sees each other as well as our customers as family, working hard every day to deliver the best experience possible!!
